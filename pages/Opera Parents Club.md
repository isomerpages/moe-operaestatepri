<h5>Introduction to Opera Parents Club</h5><br>

<img style="width:250px;height:200px;" alt="HTML tutorial" src="/images/2026/Opc/OPC_Logo.jpg">

<p align="justify">Opera Parents Club (OPC) is Opera Estate Primary School’s Parent Support Group. It is open to all parents of children enrolled in the school and provides opportunities for volunteering and peer support by:</p>

●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Enhancing our OEPS students’ school life &amp; wellbeing<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Nurturing a vibrant, fun and happy environment for their learning<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Providing opportunities for OEPS families to build community<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Supporting ad-hoc school activities

<p align="justify">The three cubes of the logo represent <b>The Students,</b> <b>The Educators</b> and <b>The Parents</b> of Opera Estate Primary School.</p>

<p align="justify">The unified cubes form a triumvirate of mutual support, synergy and achievement. Choose Your Own Adventure! </p>

<p align="justify">Involvement in OPC activities is entirely based on each members’ interests and availability. <b>There is no minimum requirement</b>. Just volunteer and attend events when you are able to! OPC is for parents, by parents. Opportunities include:</p>

●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ad-Hoc Volunteering (as and when it works for you!)<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Playhub (recess craft and play)<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Reading Club &amp; Little Library (reading programmes)<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Safety Squad (road safety<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; For Parents, By Parents

Why Get Involved?

●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Closer connection to the school<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; First-hand updates<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Children love seeing parents around school<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Increased positive outcomes for the children<br>
●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Develop rapport and mutual support with other OEPS parents
